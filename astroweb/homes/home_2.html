The Berkeley Astrophysical Fluid Dynamics Group uses state-of-the-art numerical techniques to study the behavior of gas in the interstellar medium, including physical effects such as self-gravity, magnetic fields, and radiative transfer. Using these methods, we have investigated supernova remnants, star formation, shock-cloud interactions, and similar phenomena demanding accurate, high-resolution computation.
